# Optional tweaks

## Disable IPv6

*If you don't know what IPv6 is, check out my handy dandy [What is IPv6?](../infrastructure/faq.md#what-is-ipv6) section of my FAQ.*

I've put this in the *optional tweaks* section of my guide, as I generally would not recommend you do this, **unless** you're facing issues related to IPv6.

I was having issues with one of my services, and no amount of troubleshooting seemed to be able to fix the issue. This was until, as a last ditched effort, I decided to try disabling IPv6 on my homelab, and the issue went away. It's not an ideal solution, however, I weighed up the pros and cons, and as of right now, I don't think IPv6 is **essential** for *my* homelab setup.

This is a band-aid solution. For me, it temporarily fixes the issue so my homelab can work as intended, and allows me to come back at a later date to revisit and attempt to fix the issue.
